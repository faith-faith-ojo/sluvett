About SLUVET

Craft without
compromise.

Founded in 2019, SLUVET was built on a single principle — that clothing should carry weight. Not in mass, but in intention. Every piece is designed to outlast trends, seasons, and noise.

We work with a small network of specialist manufacturers across Europe. No shortcuts. No fast fashion cycles. Just craft, repeated until it's right.
